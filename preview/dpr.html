Rolls-Royce Creates Bespoke Picnic Hamper for Phantom Zenith Collection

By Ed Jones
August 18, 2016
Shares0

Rolls-Royce announced in May that they would be creating the Phantom Zenith Collection. This collection commemorates the Phantom Drophead Coupe and Phantom Coupe though a bespoke collection that is the ultimate in luxury. To accompany this collection, Rolls-Royce has unveiled something special: a bespoke-commissioned picnic hamper.

Yes, that’s right, the Zenith Collection will now come with one of the finest picnic hampers created by Rolls-Royce themselves.

Each hamper will be numbered to match each owner’s Phantom Zenith Collection model. Each hamper is created from American Walnut and natural grain leather. They come finished in three color combinations, matching the interior of the cars (Ardent Red leather and black leather piping from the 1929 Phantom II, Navy Blue and Arctic White leather piping from the 1925 Phantom II and black Anthracite leather and Seashell piping from the 1911 Silver Ghost). In the box is lead crystal wine glasses, handmade stainless steel cutlery, Rolls-Royce embroidered napkins, American Walnut chopping board and unique crockery.

This all is definitely enough for one of the finest picnics ever.
