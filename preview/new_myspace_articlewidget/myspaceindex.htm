The electronic artist wants you to tap into your spiritual self.

Keith Varick, better known as Varick onstage, is a spiritual person. So he takes that approach when it comes to his music. Creating ambient yet lush soundscapes, he wants to take you along on this journey, especially on his latest song, "Surrender."

As the title suggests, the song is full of bouncing bass beats and spacey sounds, which Varick's vocals sit comfortable on top of.

"This track is about surrendering your ego in order to find a deeper meaning within yourself," the New York City-based electronic artist tells Myspace.

"Surrender" is off his upcoming album, Veda I, which releases on September 9.
