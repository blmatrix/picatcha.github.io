For single moms, that bond is only amplified and becomes unlike anything else you know. As the child of a single mom, I can't even begin to describe it to you, but I know it when I see it -- and it's definitely something worth celebrating!

We've rounded up 13 super-cool celeb moms who make our insides flutter of fondness over the incredible bond they have with their kiddos, and guess what? They're all single mamas.
