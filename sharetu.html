Season 7 of Game of Thrones is Going to have How Many Episodes!?

2nd June 2016 Game of Thrones, Got, Jon Snow, Season 7
  • Facebook
    112
  • Twitter
  • Pinterest
    0


You know how it is, you wait for what seems like a lifetime for the next Game of Thrones series, and then in ten short weeks it’s all over for another year. But I’m afraid the news is about to get a whole lot worse…

Director Jack Bender, who has directed episode five and six of this series, revealed that there will not be ten episodes next year, but just seven! And here was me thinking that George RR Martin was the cruel one!

When asked by VF Hollywood whether he would direct any episodes in season seven, Bender said “I don’t know the answer to that”  before letting slip that “They’re only doing seven, and they’ve got their regulars who have done is forever”. This news is made even worse by the fact that showrunners David Benioff and DB Weiss confessed that they think “we’re down to our final 13 episodes after this season… That’s the guess, though nothing is yet set in stone, but that’s what we’re looking at.”

So if you thought George RR was cruel is doesn’t look like GoT producers David Benioff and DB Wiess are any nicer, although it might not all be bad news…
