Obama’s State Department is releasing the emails to or from three senior Hillary Clinton aides with a very slow timeline, 75 years.

The Republican National Committee is demanding they be released before the general election in November.

I am afraid to see what the compromise looks like…20 years? Oh brother!

The Hill reports:

Two weeks after the Obama administration claimed it would take at least 75 years to process the documents demanded as part of an open records lawsuit, the RNC filed a court motion accusing the government of using “stall tactics” and misleading legal tricks.
